COVID-19 UPDATE:

To our valued customers

As you have likely heard, the Government has given the green light for Physiotherapy clinics to open their doors to non-urgent in person visits as of May 4, 2020 by following specific strict guidelines and precautions.
Obtaining appropriate PPE (personal protective equipment) and following the guidelines and precautions recommended by the Government, Public Health officials, the College of Physiotherapy of Manitoba, and the Canadian Physiotherapy Association is our main focus to ensure the safety of all our clients, families and staff.
At this time, we will remain closed for in person clinic visits, but will continue to provide MOBILE (in your home/gym/workplace) and TeleRehab/Virtual Physiotherapy visits. WE ALSO UNDERSTAND THAT YOU STILL MAY BE LIVING IN PAIN, REQUIRE EXERCISE PRESCRIPTION OR NEED HELP MANAGING YOUR CONDITION. WE ARE HAPPY TO STILL BE OPEN FOR VIRTUAL PHYSIOTHERAPY VISITS. THESES ARE SECURE 2 WAY VIDEO CONSULTS TO ALLOW US TO PERFORM A VISUAL FUNCTIONAL ASSESSMENT AND PROVIDE EXERCISES, SELF MANAGEMENT TECHNIQUES, AND RECOMMENDATIONS TO HELP YOU MANAGE YOUR PAIN, INJURY, PRENATAL/POSTPARTUM, OR POST SURGERY JOURNEY.
We will continue to evaluate an appropriate opening date in the future for in person clinic visits, as we are able to obtain the appropriate PPE and implement the safety guidelines necessary to ensure safe care for all. We will be looking at a slow, gradual opening for in person visits, starting with a limited schedule and spaced out appointment times.
Online booking will be posted on our website one week in advance and with a limited capacity. If you do not see a time slot that works for you, please do not hesitate to call or email us, as other options may be available that are not showing up online.
If you are unsure about TeleRehab/Virtual Physiotherapy visits, please contact us or go online to book a FREE 15 min virtual visit to trial the video system and discuss with the Physiotherapist if this option is appropriate for your situation.

Services
We offer a variety of services. If you don't see what you need below please contact us.



NO CLIENT OVERLAPMOBILE ASSESSMENT/TREATMENT

All appointments are spent with your physiotherapist focusing only on you. Our goal is to spend more one on one time with our clients providing thorough assessments, treatments, and education. No overlapping of clients during their sessions.
For some individuals, leaving their place of residence may not be an option. Our physiotherapist will come to you if you live within Bangalore. Assessments and treatments can be completed in the privacy of your own home.













SOFT TISSUE RELEASE/JOINT MOBILIZATIONVIRTUAL PHYSIOTHERAPY

Active and passive muscle release and joint mobilizations can assist with increasing range of motion prior to exercise and can relieve pain and discomfort in the short term. Following soft tissue release or joint mobilization, it is beneficial to strengthen these increased ranges of motion for long term results.
The use of web based video conferencing systems to allow you and your physiotherapist to connect via video on your computer or smart phone via web camera to receive physiotherapy services at a distance when an in-person visit is not possible.















HOME EXERCISE PROGRAMS

All clients will be provided with a home exercise program to empower them to take part in their rehabilitation and fitness goals. The goal is to never stop moving. Modifications and alternative exercises will be provided so the client can remain active while working through their recovery.
